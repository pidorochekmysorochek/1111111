How I judge casinos

The stuff I actually care about before depositing

Because a shiny homepage means basically nothing if withdrawals are a mess

What matters most

  • Cashout speed, especially on crypto withdrawals
  • Clear KYC rules before you deposit real money
  • Real game providers, not some mystery slot catalog
  • Support that does not vanish when you ask about withdrawals
  • Bonus terms that do not feel like a legal trap

Red flags

  • No real license info or company name anywhere useful
  • Footer badges that do not link to anything verifiable
  • Too many complaints about stalled cashouts
  • Wild bonus claims with tiny unreadable terms
  • Support replies that sound copied and go nowhere
